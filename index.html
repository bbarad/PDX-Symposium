About the Symposium

The 2026 Portland CryoEM Symposium brings together leading researchers, students, and industry professionals to explore the latest developments in cryo-electron microscopy. Join us for a day of cutting-edge science, networking, and collaboration in the heart of Portland, Oregon.

Event Highlights

  • Presentations from expert trainees and faculty in CryoEM
  • Poster sessions showcasing the latest research from labs around Oregon
  • Networking opportunities with peers and industry leaders
